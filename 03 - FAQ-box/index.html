Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship to my country?
We ship worldwide to over 180 countries. You can select your shipping destination at the top left corner of our website.
Which payment methods does the shop accept?
We accept American Express, Bancontact / Mister Cash, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, Maestro, MasterCard (Eurocard), UnionPay, Visa, PayPal and Apple Pay.
Can I change or cancel my order?
We strive to ship orders promptly, so please get in touch with our Customer Service as soon as possible, if you would like to edit or cancel your purchase.
How soon can I get my order?
All the items that are in stock will be shipped next business day. You can see the estimated delivery date on each product page and at checkout.
What is the return policy
The customers have the right to return or exchange any product within 30 days of delivery. You can register your return by signing in to My Account and selecting the order from the Previous orders list.
